About

28 years of tradition, passion, and flavor

Our story

A tradition carried through generations

Gigo Restaurant began its story in 1998 as a small family tavern in the heart of Belgrade.

From the very start, the founders had a clear vision: to create a place where guests would feel at home, where every dish would be made with care, and where tradition would be valued above all.

Today, after years of experience, Gigo Restaurant remains a symbol of authenticity and quality. While we've grown and refreshed our space, we've stayed true to our roots — every dish is still prepared using original family recipes, and every guest is treated like a member of our family.
