Our story

About us

Gigo Restaurant was founded in 1998 as a symbol of family tradition and a love for great food.

For generations, we have cherished authentic Serbian recipes — each dish is prepared with care, without shortcuts. Our mission is to offer more than a meal: a warm welcome, the comfort of home, and flavors you remember.

We use fresh, seasonal ingredients from local suppliers.
